Big Island

Hawaii

A nice sunny paradise with beaches, waterfalls, forests, volcanos, and more. There are many places to visit and things to do. A trip here would be fun and explorative. You can snorkel, scuba dive, see whales, hike, see volcanic rock, enjoy a day at the scenic beaches, and so many other fun activities.

Continue Reading

Rome

Italy

Pizza, pasta, gelato, ice cream, and historic locations is Rome. You can visit the Colloseum, fontana de trevi, pantheon, roman forum, spanish steps, piazza navona, mouth of truth, and so so much more. You could spend days visiting all the cool places the city has to offer. You can buy all sorts of items such as paintings, snowglobes, sun umbrellas, and more from street vendors. But be very careful with your possesions around the vendors who sell the umbrellas.

Bucharest

Romania

Bucharest is filled with cool places to visit/see like the Palace of Parliment, Arcul de Triumf, Herăstrău Lake, Cărturești Carusel, StoneMania Bijou, and so so much more! There is one other place (NOT IN BUCHAREST), Bran Castle (IN BRAN) which is an amazing place to visit in Romania.

Seattle

Washington

Where do i start? There are so many places to visit like the Space Needle, Museum of Pop Culture, Pike Place Market, Seattle Aquarium, Woodland Park Zoo, The Museum of Flight, The Seattle Great Wheel, The Gum Wall, Ballard (Hiram M. Chittenden) Locks, and so so so so so much more!

Los Angeles

California

Beaches, palm trees, and nice weather. Los Angeles has many nice places to visit including the Hollywood Sign, Griffith Observatory, Disneyland Park, Santa Monica Pier, Universal Studios Hollywood, Hollywood Walk of Fame, TCL Chinese Theatre, Hollywood Boulevard, Universal Studio Tour, there are of course other places to visit too.

Paris

France

The Eiffel tower, castles, baguettes, macarons. There are so many other famous and cool places to visit like the Louvre, Arc de Triomphe, Catacombs of Paris, Panthéon, Louvre Pyramid, The Thinker, Sewers Museum, and again there's still more!
